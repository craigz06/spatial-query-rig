delta). Authentic video clusters 0.1–0.3. Synthetic scatters 1.0–3.0. Zero 
overlap in validated corpus.

**Architecture:** Three-pass — Physics suite (ANA) → Scene Logic critic → 
Synthesis (SCO). One confirmed contradiction disqualifies authenticity regardless 
of physics scores.

  prosthetic for stateless AI)

  centroid drift analysis)

- J-M Effect (Johansson-Muybridge Effect) — sequential frame delivery at ~18fps 
  produces temporal phase transition across AI substrates

**Validated corpus:** N=20+ authentic/synthetic. CoV separation: order of 
magnitude. Next milestone: N=200 batch test using FaceForensics++ corpus.

**Honest limitation:** Social media compression degrades the Delta CoV signal.
